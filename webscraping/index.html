List of all countries with their capitals and currencies
April 24 2024

List of Countries, Capitals, and Currencies of the world: 

 

In a lot of MBA exams, you may get questions based on countries, especially their capitals and currencies. You will find these questions, particularly in IIFT, where it is usually given in the form of the match the following (4-5 options). Such questions are easy to score, but you can't get them right on pure guesses since they can be a bit confusing at times. So, here we have for you a list of all capitals and currencies of countries worldwide in one single table for your ease of learning.
